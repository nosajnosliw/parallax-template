I AM NOSAJ

I was born in a car in Cleveland, OH and have been hacking life ever since. Currently, a resident of Bed-Stuy Brooklyn, I swim like a fish, eat like a bear, and am generally delighted by hard workouts, eggs & sausage, Retail Checkout UI done right, Paleo Goodness, Soundcloud UI updates and good techno/house music.

My natural sense of curiosity, unique sense of humor and problem solving skills allow me to learn and grow from challenges. Empathy also enables me to step outside myself to enhance my understanding of the user, his/her goals, and the processes used to reach them. Thinking with the user's thumbs is a fun way to begin dreaming up other digital human factors, beyond my own. I am always trying to expand my understanding of our collective and individual user goals.
